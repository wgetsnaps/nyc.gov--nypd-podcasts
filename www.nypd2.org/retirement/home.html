As Police Commissioner, I’d like to welcome you to the NYPD Retiree Website. This site is dedicated to assisting retired members of the service. You have earned the respect both of the city you served and the brave men and women who follow in your footsteps.

After the terrorist attacks on September 11th, many of you enthusiastically responded by assisting the Department and your fellow citizens at sites throughout the city. Thousands of retired members responded to a follow-up survey, indicating a willingness to help under any circumstances. You showed that even in retirement, your heart remains with your fellow officers and New York City. Your training, dedication and experience proved invaluable and can certainly be an asset in the future.

Here, you will find answers to frequently asked questions (FAQ) about volunteering and retirement support. I invite you to explore the links page to find resources regarding your retirement. Remember, your connection to the Department doesn’t end when you turn in your shield.

In the future, we may seek your assistance for other events. A secured section of this website allows you to provide and update contact information to assist the Department during an emergency.
